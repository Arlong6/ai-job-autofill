## Detailed Description

AI Job Autofill saves you hours on repetitive job applications. Fill out entire Greenhouse and Lever application forms in a single click -- name, email, phone, work history, education, links, and even EEO demographic questions.

Stop copying and pasting the same information into every application. Set up your profile once, and AI Job Autofill handles the rest across 20+ supported field types on boards.greenhouse.io and jobs.lever.co, the two most popular applicant tracking systems used by tech companies worldwide.


FEATURES

One-Click Autofill
Open any Greenhouse or Lever job application, click the autofill button, and watch your details populate instantly. Handles text fields, dropdowns, file uploads, and multi-select options.

AI Match Score
Before you apply, see how well your profile aligns with the job requirements. The match score analyzes the job description against your experience and skills so you can prioritize the roles where you have the strongest fit.

AI-Powered Cover Letters
Generate a tailored cover letter for each application in seconds. The AI reads the job posting and writes a cover letter that highlights your most relevant experience -- no more staring at a blank page.

Application Tracker
Keep a record of every position you apply to. See your application history at a glance, track which companies you have applied to, and stay organized throughout your job search without needing a separate spreadsheet.

Built-In Profile Editor
Enter your information once in the extension's profile form. Your details are saved locally and ready to use on every application.


HOW IT WORKS

1. Install the extension and fill out your profile.
2. Navigate to any job application on Greenhouse or Lever.
3. Click the AI Job Autofill icon to open the side panel.
4. Review your match score and generate a cover letter if needed.
5. Hit autofill and submit your application.


PRIVACY AND PRICING

Your data never leaves your browser. All profile information and application history are stored locally on your device. There are no external servers, no accounts to create, and no data collection.

AI features (cover letter generation and match scoring) are powered by the Gemini API using your own API key. You bring your own key, so there is no subscription, no paid tier, and no usage limits imposed by us. The extension is completely free.


WHO IS THIS FOR

Job seekers applying to tech companies through Greenhouse and Lever job boards. Whether you are applying to five roles or fifty, AI Job Autofill removes the friction so you can focus on finding the right opportunity.
