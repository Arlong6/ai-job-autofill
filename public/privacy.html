Privacy Policy

AI Job Autofill — Chrome Extension

1. Overview

AI Job Autofill is a Chrome extension that helps users auto-fill job applications. This policy explains what data the extension handles, how it is stored, and how it is used. We are committed to transparency and to minimizing data collection.

2. Data We Collect and Store

All data is provided voluntarily by you within the extension interface. The extension stores the following information locally on your device using chrome.storage.local:

  • Personal details: name, email address, phone number
  • Education history
  • Work experience
  • Skills and qualifications
  • EEO demographic information (e.g., gender, race/ethnicity, veteran status) — provided at your discretion
  • Your Gemini API key (stored locally, never transmitted to us)

The extension does not collect passwords, financial information, browsing history, analytics, telemetry, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

3. How Your Data Is Used

  • Auto-filling applications: Your stored profile data is used to populate fields on supported job application sites (boards.greenhouse.io and jobs.lever.co).
  • AI-powered features: When you use match scoring or cover letter generation, the job description and a summary of your profile/skills are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing.

Your data is never used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than the features described above.

4. Data Storage and Sync

All data resides on your device in Chrome's local storage. If you have Chrome Sync enabled in your browser, Chrome may sync extension storage data across your signed-in devices using Google's infrastructure. This is controlled entirely by your Chrome settings, not by the extension.

No data is sent to any server owned or operated by the extension developer. We do not operate servers, databases, or backend services of any kind.

5. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service the extension communicates with is Google's Gemini API, and only when you explicitly trigger an AI feature (match scoring or cover letter generation). This communication uses your own API key, which you provide. Data sent to the Gemini API is subject to Google's API Terms of Service.

The extension does not use cookies, analytics services, crash reporters, or any other third-party tracking tools.

6. Permissions

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — To save your profile data locally.
  • activeTab — To interact with the current job application page when you activate the extension.
  • scripting — To inject content scripts that fill form fields on supported job boards.

Content scripts run only on boards.greenhouse.io and jobs.lever.co. The extension does not access or read any other websites.

7. Data Security

Your data is stored using Chrome's built-in extension storage APIs, which isolate data to the extension's sandboxed environment. Your Gemini API key is stored locally and is transmitted only to Google's API endpoints over HTTPS when you use AI features. We recommend keeping your browser and extension up to date.

8. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • You can view, edit, or delete any stored information through the extension's settings.
  • Uninstalling the extension permanently removes all locally stored data.
  • You can revoke your Gemini API key at any time through the Google AI Studio console.

9. GDPR Compliance

Because AI Job Autofill stores all personal data locally on your device and does not transmit it to any developer-controlled server, data processing is performed entirely under your control. When AI features are used, data sent to Google's Gemini API is processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policies and terms. Users in the European Economic Area retain all rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the rights of access, rectification, erasure, and data portability — all of which can be exercised by managing or removing the extension.

10. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
